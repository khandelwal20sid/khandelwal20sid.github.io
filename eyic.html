e-Parking Management

The project was made in order to solve the real time problem of mismanagement of parking in this country. Our idea was to create a web application that would allow the user to pre-book the slots on a single click. The in-app feature of google maps would help in making people aware of their nearest parking location. Implementing the idea on a vast level could definitely help in reduction of chaotic situations and would also contribute towards smart city project.
