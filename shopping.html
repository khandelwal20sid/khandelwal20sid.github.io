e-Commerce Clothing Site

This project was the part of my learnings. After completing the cource of javascrpt, I tried this site and successfully made it. It was actually my very first self made websitem, not a complete website tho!

You can add the item in cart and remove it from there. Same item can't be added to cart again, you'll get an alert message in that case. Other than this cart feature, you can also search for your item. The list will get updated after each word typed and you'll get your item. Price Filter with max and min price will display the items in the range.
